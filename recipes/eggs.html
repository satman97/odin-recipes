Eggs

A plate of scrambled eggs with chives on top

In this recipe you will make the most delicious scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • chives
  • butter
  • salt

Steps

  1. Heat the butter in a small nonstick pan
  2. Add 2 eggs to the hot butter and start gently mixing
  3. While eggs are still just a touch underdone take off the heat to finish cooking
  4. Sprinkle with chives as desired
