Popcorn

A bucket of buttery popcorn

In this recipe you will make the most delicious bucket of buttery popcorn. By the end of it your tounge will be numb from the salt.

Ingredients

  • popcorn
  • oil
  • butter
  • salt

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in your popcorn maker for 2 minutes
  2. Add 1/4 cup of popcorn kernels to hot oil
  3. Wait until there is no more popping
  4. Season popcorn liberally with salt and butter
