Grilled Cheese

3 half grilled cheese sandwhiches stacked on each other. Where did the third one go?

In this recipe you will make the most delicious grilled cheese sandwhich.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • cheese
  • butter
  • salt

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  2. Place one slice of bread butter side down in hot skillet
  3. Add cheese and last slice of bread to sandwhich
  4. Flip sandwhich and season with salt as desired
